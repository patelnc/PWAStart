The Pop Up Restaurant Concept

What are you waiting for?! Our Pop Up Restaurants have been open for over a year, so what are you waiting for?! Local chefs are featured in the most unique dining locations in the Waterloo Area. A gourmet 2-5 course meal will be served, along with any or all of the following: live music, entertainment, tasteful decor, take-home treats, and, of course, the social atmosphere. Do you want to know more? Scroll down to find out more.

Let me walk you through the process:

  1. You pick a date that works for you from our event calendar
  2. Then head onto our online ticket platform and grab your tickets
  3. Sign up for our mailing list, so you don't miss out on upcoming tickets and ticket specials HERE
  4. Sit, look pretty, and wait…..cause that's pretty much all the info we give you for now. But hey, you're supporting local, you're gonna eat fresh and it's going to be pretty amazing, so you should feel pretty spectacular right now!
  5. Tic toc…tic toc….
  6. The night before the pop up, we'll email you the location! That's right…the secret is revealed!
  7. Just so you know,some of our events are BYOB. We make sure to tell you this on the ticket and event page, so keep a lookout.
  8. 6PM…arrive at your location…mingle a little, eat A LOT….have fun!
